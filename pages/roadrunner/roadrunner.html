Introduction

Welcome to RoadRunner, this is a Mobile fitness social media app that uses your GPS location to track your time, pace, and distance on your run. With RoadRunner you're able to follow, like, and comment on your friends runs.

Idea behind RoadRunner

Running is one of my favorite things to do in order to de-stress. The app that I use to log my runs is Strava and I love their UI which is so easy to use and understand. So, I wanted to challenge myself and try and build a fitness social media app that is usable.

Role in RoadRunner

For this team project I took lead developing the running feature that access the user geolocation informaiton.

Challenges

Major Challenge on this Project

The major challenge for me with RoadRunner was learning geolocation and trying to get the users coordinates updated in a timely manner. When accessing the coordinates of a user it's being stored in an Array and a line is drawn from coordinate to coordinate. However, I was experiencing issues with dupilicate coordinates being stored, due to this the line was not being drawn. So I had to create a filter option that will remove any duplicate coordinates.
