Introduction

Welcome to Just Comedy, a personality test through a user-focused app to match stand-up comedian preferences with an autogenerated selection based on the user data and algorithms from a curated database of content.

Idea behind Just Comedy

I'm a huge fan of comedy and I love to share that passion with my friends, however when I would give recommmendations to my firends I would have to asked them alot of questions. Due to their unique answers I would have to really think which comedian would best suit their humor.

So, I came up with the idea of creating a quiz that will ask all the questions I would normally ask and generate the perfect match for them.

Role in Just Comedy

For this project I desgined and coded this full stack application.

UI Experience

Getting Started

Once the user is ready to begin their quiz they're greeted with the classic comedy club brick wall. I wanted the UI to simulate the user being in a comedy club.

Quiz Page

The user will have to go over 11 hand crafted questions that will disect their comedy style.

Results

After the user answers the questions which is a point system in the back end. The score will be incorporated into an algorithm that checks which comedian score is closest to the user score.

Comedian Profile

If the user wants to know more information about the comedian such as which podcast they appear on, social media links, and most importantly what their specials are called.

Challenges

Major Challenge on this Project

The major challenge I encountered was not finding any comedian data or API's that contains information about comedians. So, I had to create a database with specific information for every comedian such as specials, podcast appearences, social media links, and TV/Movie appearences.

Styling The Curtain

Trying to get the curtain animation to be responsive and smooth was hard. So, I had to do alot of research on Key Frames and how animation works with code.
