Introduction

Jot is a personalized, versatile logger application crafted specifically for Gen Z. It is designed to accommodate their unique preferences for tracking mental health, social experiences, and daily events.

Idea behind Jot

The initial vision for Jot was to create an application that supports mental health tracking. However, we envisioned a broader scope, allowing for a space where any thought, experience, or burst of creativity could be captured.

Our app enables users to "Jot" down anything from a brilliant movie idea to a memorable or even challenging day, enhancing personal reflection and data visualization over time.

Observing that Gen Z often logs their moments using basic notes apps or uses specific apps like sobriety trackers, we were inspired to develop a more comprehensive logger app. Jot not only captures these moments but also organizes and generates insightful data automatically.

UI Experience

Getting Started

Upon starting the app, users are greeted by their unique selection of personalized categories that they have created, setting the stage for a tailored experience right from the beginning.

Sub Category Page

Within the main category, users can add subcategories that are directly linked to their primary interests. This structure simplifies the process of tracking, finding, and modifying categories, making organization seamless and intuitive.

Create Sub Cateogry

When users wish to add a subcategory, they simply need to provide a title and, optionally, a description. We don't require any further details about events unless the user chooses to include them. Additionally, users have the option to add a count to each subcategory, enhancing the tracking and organization of their entries.

Data Page

Finally, users can review all their statistics, selecting any category they wish to analyze. This feature allows for a comprehensive overview of their personal data, tailored to their specific interests and needs.
