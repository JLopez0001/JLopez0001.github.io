Introduction

Jot is a personalized, versatile logger application named "Jot" designed to cater to Gen Z's unique preferences for tracking mental health, social experiences, and daily events.

Idea behind Jot

We wanted to create an application to help people track their mental health. We did not want to be bound by this and thus decided to expand the idea to any thoughts, experiences or a space to be utilized with imagination. Our app allows you to "Jot" down a great movie idea, a horrible date, or an amazing experience and you can increase the occurrences and view your data.

Gen Z nowadays log their moments on notes app or use sobriety apps to count. So we decided to create a logger app that creates the data for you.

UI Experience

Getting Started

The user is greeted by their unique selection of personalized caategories that they create.

Sub Category Page

Based on the main category the user can add sub categories that ties into the main categpry. That way tracking their categories becomes easy to find and modify.

Create Sub Cateogry

When the user wants to add a sub category all they need to include is a title and a description if they want. We dont require the user to input any details of what occured if they dont want to. The user can also add a count to the subcategory.

Data Page

Finally the user can review all their stats based on whatever category they want.

Challenges

Major Challenge on this Project

We went straight into creating a full-stack app a day after a final unit of learning MERN. The learning curve was that we had two days to code a full app with authorization, testing the API, and debug as a team a day after we finished our unit. We consumed a vast scale of knowledge and balanced a BootCamp project while also creating an app for this hackathon. Overall, the challenges we ran into were time management and the learning curve of being new to this. However, our team was very strong in terms of team spirit and our level of hard work.

What's Next For Jot

We want to be able to clean up the CSS in terms of how we organize our wireframe. We will continue working on this app through react native and deploy it on the app store. Many of our friends of the new generation love to count their moments in life and share them on TikTok and this app would be very useful to them.
